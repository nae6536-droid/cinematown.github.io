关于我们 About Us

电子纸镇 Cinematown 是一个常驻中国杭州的独立放映组织，致力于在当代语境中探索影像的观看与讨论方式。我们以 小规模、手工策划 的方式组织每月1-2场放映，内容涵盖纪录片、实验影像、艺术短片等多种形式。

我们采用 游牧式放映 策略，在杭州不同文化空间中流动播映。所有作品均获得导演授权，强调创作者的表达权与影像本身的完整性。我们支持独立影像，希望建立一个观众与作品、创作者之间平等、开放的交流现场。

电子纸镇不是一家固定影院，而是一块移动银幕，一座在城市中不断临时搭建的小型影像小镇。

Cinematown is a small-scale independent screening collective based in Hangzhou, China, dedicated to exploring how moving images are watched and discussed in contemporary contexts.

We curate 1–2 carefully programmed screenings each month, focusing on documentaries, experimental films, and artist videos.

As a nomadic and small-scale project, we move across diverse cultural venues in the city. All works are screened with permission from the filmmakers, emphasizing the integrity of authorship and the spirit of independent cinema.

We aim to create a modest but open space for equal dialogue between audiences and moving images.

Cinematown is not a fixed cinema, but a traveling screen — a temporary town quietly built around films.
